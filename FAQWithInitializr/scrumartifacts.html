Scrum Artifacts

Scrum has certain artifacts - representations of work or value which help to quantify the Scrum process. The artifacts are defined such that everyone has an understanding of what the object is and what purpose(s) it serves.

What is the Product Backlog?

The Product Backlog is a list providing all of the work to be done, in order of priority. It is managed by the Product Owner in the Scrum team.

What is the Sprint Backlog, and what makes it different?

The Sprint Backlog and the Product Backlog are similar in that the Sprint Backlog is a selection of Product Backlog items that are to be performed as part of a sprint - the entirety of things to be done to reach the Sprint Goal. This helps to make work to be done during a sprint visible to all Development Team members.
