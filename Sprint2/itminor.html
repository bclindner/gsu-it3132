IT as a Minor

Tech-Savvy Applicants Wanted

As a minor, information technology complements a degree by giving students knowledge of information technology and an understanding of information and communication challenges. IT complements many majors because all organizations need people who understand information resources and information technology. Students who minor in information management and technology increase their employability in a variety of careers.

Potential Courses

Get More From Your Degree

In order to pursue a career in a technological field, you may have thought about a minor in Information Technology. By learning more about the types of work options you can attain by completing a minor in this field, you can determine whether doing so will help you accomplish the career goals you have set for yourself. In declaring a minor in Information technology, students could take multiple courses, some of which might include:

  • Introduction to IT

  • Web Page Development

  • IT Infrastructure

  • Web App Design

  • Web Software
