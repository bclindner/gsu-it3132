Specializations & Minors

Your Path to Success

As with most other universities, Georgia Southern offers you the ability to customize the courses taken in your degree to better suit your needs. There are two important ways to do this:

  • Specializations are 9-hour groups of major courses you can choose to better suit the degree program for different types of IT work, such as Web development and system administration.

  • Minors, or second disciplines/concentrations, are groups of courses to be taken alongside major classes which complement them.

Specializations

A Degree with Choices

Georgia Southern lets you choose one of four specializations as part of your degree, which can better prepare you for a future career:

  • Systems Development and Support,

  • Web and Multimedia Foundations,

  • Information Management, and

  • Networking and Datacenter Administration.

These specializations come with three courses you must take as part of your degree. The courses in a specialization help to better familiarize you with more specific aspects of information technology, depending on what you choose.
